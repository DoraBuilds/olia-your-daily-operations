## Usage Notes

- Keep at least one ring-width of clear space around the mark.
- Prefer dark or ink surfaces for the gradient mark.
- On light backgrounds, use the dark mark or dark lockup.
- Do not stretch the mark non-proportionally.
- Do not rotate the mark unless motion is intentional in animation.
- Keep gradients smooth; avoid hard stop bands and neon oversaturation.
